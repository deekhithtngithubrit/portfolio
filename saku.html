"Financial News Sentimental Analysis Based On Reviews Using ML"

Financial news sentiment analysis based on reviews using machine learning is the process of automatically identifying and categorizing opinions expressed in financial news articles or reviews. This involves analyzing the text of the news article or review to determine the sentiment expressed, whether it is positive, negative, or neutral. The analysis is performed using machine learning algorithms that are trained on large datasets of labeled data. The resulting sentiment analysis can be used to gain insights into market trends and help traders and investors make more informed decisions. This application of machine learning in finance is becoming increasingly important as the volume of financial news data continues to grow.

Project 4

"Fruits Spoilage detection using IOT"

Fruit spoilage detection using IoT involves monitoring the environmental conditions of fruit storage facilities using IoT devices such as temperature and humidity sensors. The data collected from these sensors is then analyzed to detect any changes that may indicate spoilage, such as an increase in temperature or humidity levels. This information can be used to alert farmers or warehouse managers of potential spoilage, allowing them to take corrective action before it becomes a major problem. Additionally, this technology can also help reduce waste and improve the overall quality of fruit production.

Project 5

A baby incubator using embedded system is a device that helps to provide a controlled environment for premature babies to ensure their survival and growth. It involves the use of embedded systems to monitor and control various parameters such as temperature, humidity, and oxygen levels inside the incubator. This ensures that the baby is kept in a safe and stable environment, helping to prevent complications and increase their chances of survival. The use of an embedded system allows for precise control of the incubator's parameters, making it an essential tool in neonatal care.
